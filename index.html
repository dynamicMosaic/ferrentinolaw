Orange County Criminal Defense Lawyers

Defending Clients in Costa Mesa & All Surrounding Areas

Ferrentino & Associates is a criminal defense firm that offers a wealth of knowledge and a level of intimacy that is hard to find anywhere else. When faced with criminal charges, you need to know that you have an attorney who will truly take the time to study your case and build a solid defense. You need a lawyer who is recognized and respected in the courtroom. When you come to Ferrentino & Associates, you don't just get a single attorney—you get a team of respected and trusted professionals.

Attorney Ferrentino is a Certified Criminal Law Specialist and has more than 20 years of trial experience, as well as a dedicated team of legal professionals behind her. As a boutique firm, Attorney Correen Ferrentino works collaboratively with paralegals, law clerks, and investigators to craft compelling and thorough defenses.

Our lawyers take pride in counseling clients and advocating for them, ensuring that they understand what their case entails and are fully informed every step of the way.

Why Hire Us

More Than 500 Cases Tried in State & Federal Courts

Having focused her practice exclusively on criminal defense for more than two decades, Correen Ferrentino has a wealth of insight to draw from when defending the rights and freedoms of her clients. She does not back down from complicated cases and has a long list of success stories to show for it. She has tried more than 50 jury trials and more than 400 court trials and evidentiary hearings in both state and federal courts, including homicides, serious felonies, federal crimes, and writs of habeas corpus.

The team at Ferrentino & Associates is able to offer the highest standard of representation to our clients in Orange County, California. Taking a collaborative approach, we strategize defenses, litigate trials, research and draft motions and appeals, and handle every aspect of our clients' cases between us.

Contact Ferrentino & Associates at (714) 613-0801 to schedule a free case evaluation with our Southern California & Orange County​ criminal defense attorneys.
